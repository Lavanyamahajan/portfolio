TravelToGetHer - Car Rental Platform

TravelToGetHer is an ecommerce platform that offers users a seamless experience for car rentals. It allows travelers to select from a wide range of vehicles for any occasion, whether it's a short trip or a long road journey.

  • Role: Full-stack Developer
  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB
  • Features:
    • User-friendly car selection and booking system
    • Wide range of vehicle categories
    • Secure payment gateway integration
    • Responsive design for both mobile and desktop devices
    • Admin dashboard for managing vehicles and bookings
